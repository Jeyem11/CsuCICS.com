Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

Information technology (IT) is the use of computers to create, process, store, retrieve, and exchange all kinds of electronic data and information. IT is typically used within the context of business operations as opposed to personal or entertainment technologies. IT is considered to be a subset of information and communications technology (ICT).

Jobs Suitable:

  • IT technician
  • IT Security Specialist
  • IT Director
  • IT Coordinator
  • Data Scientist
  • Network Engineer
  • Quality Assurance Tester
  • Applications Engineer
  • Data Quality Manager
  • Cloud System Engineer
